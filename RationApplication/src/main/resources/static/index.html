About Us

Smart-PDS Initiative

Modernizing India's Public Distribution System with cutting-edge technology

The Smart-Ration system is a digital transformation initiative under the National Food Security Act (NFSA), 2013, aimed at making ration distribution transparent, efficient, and accessible to every citizen.

AI-powered face verification prevents impersonation
Offline transaction capability for rural areas
Real-time synchronization with district servers
Duplicate claim prevention system
Instant SMS notifications to beneficiaries

How It Works

Simple 4-Step Process

From authentication to distribution - seamless and secure

1. Generate QR

Beneficiary generates unique QR code from mobile app or receives printed code

2. Face Verification

PDS officer scans QR and captures live face for AI-based verification

3. Transaction

System checks eligibility and processes ration distribution

4. Sync & Notify

Data syncs with district server; SMS confirmation sent
